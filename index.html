Dear Mumma,

Kabhi kaha nahi, par ab kehna chahti hun; Kya ho aap humare liye, ye batana chahti hun.. Aapko rota dekh, rooh kaanp jati hai; Aapko hasta dekh, jaan me jaan aati hai..

Aapke hone se lagta hai, sari duniya hai; Aapke na hone ka khayal bhi, man ko darata hai.. Kehti nahi ho aap, na jatati kabhi ho; Par is duniya me sabse jyada, aap hi hume chahti ho.. Pyari si ho aap, chhoti si cheejo me khush hone vali; Jab puchho ki kya chahiye, tab kuchh nahi batane vali..

Us kuchh nahi ka matlab, sab kuchh mante hai hum; Deni hai duniya ki sari khushi, jisse bhool jaye aap apne gam.. Ha janti hu, ki aap darti ho hume khone se; Par ab aap thoda sa, darna bhool jao; Duniya kya kahegi iski parwaah na karke, Phir se jeena seekh jao.. Hum dono rahenge sath aapke, ye jatana chahti hun; Kya ho aap humare liye, ye batana chahti hun...

With infinite love,

Shubhi & Avi
